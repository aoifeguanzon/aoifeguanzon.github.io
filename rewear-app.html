ReWear

Shopping assistant web app that helps people find secondhand and ethical alternatives to fast fashion, while connecting them to a global community of fashion-forward individuals.

01.Overview

As the Project Manager, I‚Äôm leading the development of ReWear, a sustainability-focused shopping assistant web app. It addresses the difficulty of finding stylish, affordable, and eco-friendly fashion by helping users buy and share secondhand clothing and discover ethical alternatives to fast fashion. We're releasing a demo soon and plan to launch the app this autumn.

02.Features

  1. Upload a product image or link to find secondhand dupes on platforms like Ebay and Etsy.
  2. Buy, rent or borrow items from people in your area.
  3. Browse through a feed of local products personalised to your tastes.
  4. Track your environmental impact and collect awards for completing achievements.
  5. Make friends and share your style with people all over the world.
