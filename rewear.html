ReWear

Event Branding for Virtual App Launch

01.Overview

As the Project Manager I have been creating visual assets for the upcoming virtual launch of ReWear; an eco-friendly e-commerce platform that I'm developing with a team. The web app will be released in August and I will be sharing promotional content in the coming weeks on Linkedin.

02.Goals

  1. Build excitement and recognition ahead of our launch.

  2. Encourage audience interaction through engaging launch materials.

  3. Build anticipation and interest leading up to the app release.

04.Social Media Strategy

I developed a simple and cohesive content strategy to support the virtual launch of ReWear, an eco-conscious secondhand fashion platform. I aim to build curiosity, communicate the brand’s values, and encourage early engagement on social media platforms.

Types of content

  1. Teaser posts with mockups.

  2. Behind-the-scenes content.

  3. Countdowns and launch reminders.

Visual system

  1. Shades of green and neutrals to reflect ReWear’s sustainable values.

  2. Clean, minimal layout for a modern and accessible feel.

  3. Mockups used early on to hint at the product without revealing everything.

Tone

Friendly, thoughtful and optimistic, aiming to make secondhand shopping feel easy and empowering.
