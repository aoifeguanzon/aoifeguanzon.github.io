The logo is a one colour design showing a T-shirt with a heart in the centre, surrounded by two arrows forming a circle. I chose the T-shirt because it clearly represents clothing, which is what the app focuses on. The heart adds a sense of care for the planet, for the clothes we wear, and for more thoughtful shopping. The circular arrows represent reusing and rewearing, which connects to the idea of sustainability and circular fashion. I kept the design flat and minimal to give it a fresh, uncluttered look that matches the eco-friendly values of the brand.
