01.About

I'm a Computer Science student in Dublin who is focused on full-stack development and design. I'm always looking for opportunities to create, whether that's building impactful apps, crafting unique visuals, or creating engaging content that connects with an audience.

Currently I'm the Project Manager of ReWear, a shopping assistant app that helps users discover secondhand alternatives to fast fashion and find local clothing swaps. I designed the user interface and continue to work on backend APIs while building polished frontend components. Additionally I take charge of advertising and produce materials for marketing campaigns, including the run-up to our upcoming launch.

As the Public Relations Officer of DCU Orchestra, I manage our online presence and maintain a consistent brand identity across all platforms. I create digital and physical content for the orchestra to support events, social media, and other promotional initiatives.

I'm deeply passionate about leveraging technology to build thriving communities. I'm equally driven to develop technical solutions that address global issues like climate change and economic inequality.
