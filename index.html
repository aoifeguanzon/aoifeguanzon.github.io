01.About me

I'm a Computer Science student in Dublin who is focused on full-stack development and design. I'm always looking to create, whether it's building a new app or making social content. I'm deeply passionate about leveraging technology to tackle global issues like climate change and bring communities together.

Currently I'm working as the Project Manager for ReWear, a shopping assistant app that finds you secondhand dupes for fast fashion items and helps you discover local clothing swaps. I designed its user interface and now handle backend tasks while building polished frontend components.

I'm also the Public Relations Officer of DCU Orchestra where I manage our online presence, uphold a consistent brand identity and design all our social content. At the moment I'm developing visual assets to prepare for the orchestra's upcoming season.
