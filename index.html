About

I've had a genuine passion for Computer Science ever since I was a child, making games on Scratch and Hopscotch. Since then I've continued coding in my spare time, picking up Python, Javascript, and Swift on my own at home. I'm looking forward to learning Java as my next language.

I studied Computer Science as a subject throughout secondary school, receiving a H2 in my Leaving Cert. I had lots of fun making my final project - a Python-based model using micro:bit sensors, creating a step counter which gives users tailored advice depending on the progress they made towards their wellbeing goals. This experience is what introduced me to data modelling and pattern analysis, sparking my deep interest in Machine Learning and AI, as well as in Data Analysis. During my second-level studies, I also explored Web Development and Design for the first time, which is something else I really enjoy.

Now in college, I'm constantly looking for opportunities to level up my knowledge and skillset within the field. I attend workshops, discussion panels, and networking events whenever I can. I’m working on projects that involve Python for backend logic and JavaScript for frontend development - I want to be more proficient in full-stack engineering

At the moment I am actively searching for internship opportunities to begin my career. My interests are broad, ranging from Software Engineering to Machine Learning, from Web Development to UI/UX Design, from Data Analysis to Game Development. I also want to learn more about Cybersecurity and what that involves.

I'm extremely eager for the chance to not only demonstrate my abilities in a professional setting but to engage in hands-on learning and discover new things.
