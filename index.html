01.About

I'm a Computer Science student in Dublin who is highly interested in full-stack development and design. I'm always eager to create, whether that means building meaningful apps, creating unique visuals, or producing content that engages people.

I'm currently the Project Manager of ReWear, a shopping assistant app that helps users discover secondhand alternatives to fast fashion and find local clothing swaps. I designed the user interface and continue to work on backend APIs while building polished frontend components. Additionally, I oversee advertising efforts and produce materials for marketing campaigns, including the run-up to our upcoming launch.

As the Public Relations Officer of DCU Orchestra, I manage our online presence while leading the creative direction of our brand. I create digital and print designs for the orchestra, shaping its visual identity through logos, social templates, event branding, and other creative outputs to encourage audience participation.

I'm deeply passionate about leveraging technology to build and strengthen communities. I'm equally motivated to develop solutions that address global challenges such as climate change and economic inequality.
