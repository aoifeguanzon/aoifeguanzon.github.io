DCU Orchestra

Brand identity development, creative direction, logo design, print assets, social content, post templates, event branding and promotional materials.

01.Overview

As the Public Relations Officer I'm in the process of creating a full brand identity and content system for DCU Orchestra to be used throughout the academic year. The society welcomes anyone who plays an instrument at any skill level and is interested in performing. These materials are designed for Instagram and Linkedin and printed promotions around campus.

02.Goals

  1. Grow awareness and visibility of the society online and on campus.
  2. Create a strong and welcoming visual presence that appeals to students and alumni.
  3. Make it easier and more enticing for people to join and stay engaged with the society.

05.Event Branding

Clubs & societies fair

I designed visual assets to drive signups during DCU‚Äôs Clubs & Societies Fair. These include posters, stickers, and bookmarks featuring a custom QR code linking directly to the membership page. The practical formats make it easy for students to join on the spot and keep the brand visible throughout the year.

For our stand itself, I created table toppers and banners. A phone graphic was inspired by the real iPhone QR scanning interface to reinforce our online presence, while an envelope design symbolises our invitation to join the orchestra.

One of the designs has a music staff with notes on D and C as a nod to DCU. For the 'U', I used a quarter rest; a short pause that invites others to complete the melody by joining the orchestra and playing their part.
